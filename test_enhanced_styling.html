Position Comparison Approach

Your idea of comparing specific positions is a good start! Since the strings are small, focusing on certain positions can be efficient.

💡 1. Extract Characters Approach

Extract characters at positions 0 and 2 from first string, compare with positions 1 and 3 from second string.

⏱️ Time: O(1) 💾 Space: O(1)
python 
def canBeEqual(s1, s2):
    return (s1[0] == s2[2] and s1[2] == s2[0] and
            s1[1] == s2[3] and s1[3] == s2[1])
